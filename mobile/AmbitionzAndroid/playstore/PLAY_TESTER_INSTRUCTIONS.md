# Ambitionz — Tester Instructions

Thank you for testing Ambitionz `1.0.0-beta.2` on the Rebirth `v107_LOGIC_SEARCH` runtime.

Install the app from the Google Play internal-test opt-in link while signed in with the same Google account that was added to the tester list.

## What to Test
1. Open the app.
2. Confirm the support page shows `v107_LOGIC_SEARCH`.
3. Create an account, sign out, and sign in again.
4. Start and finish one Arena clash against the bot.
5. Check XP, rewards, and saved progress after the match.
6. Start the campaign and complete an available encounter.
7. Open the collection and edit a 30-card deck.
8. Open one free beta booster and confirm the collection changes.
9. Check profile and ranking.
10. Close and reopen the app to confirm session and progress persistence.
11. Send feedback inside the app.

Use a disposable account if you are specifically testing export or permanent account deletion.

## What Feedback Should Include
- What happened?
- What did you expect?
- Which page were you on?
- Did it happen once or repeatedly?
- What phone/device did you use?
- Android version and whether the app was installed from Google Play.
- Screenshot if possible.
