Request deletion of your Ambitionz account data.

Ambitionz is currently in beta. Users may request removal of account-related data used for login, gameplay progression, deck data, collection data, match records, feedback and operational beta testing.

1. How to request deletion

To request account or data deletion, contact the Ambitionz project administrator through the available beta support or feedback channel.

You may also submit a request through the feedback page and include the title: Account Data Deletion Request.

Open Feedback Privacy Notice

2. Information to include

Include the email address or username associated with the account so the project administrator can identify the correct records.

3. Data covered by deletion

Deletion may include account profile data, login-related records, progression, deck and collection data, match records, feedback records and operational test logs associated with the account.

4. Data that may be retained temporarily

Some operational records may be retained for a limited time if required for security, abuse prevention, debugging, legal compliance or platform review obligations.

5. Beta reset notice

Because Ambitionz is in beta, test accounts, progression, rewards, matches and logs may also be reset as part of normal development and balancing operations.
