How to Play

Core Idea

Ambition TCG is a fast mobile card duel. Set your cards, choose your Intent, build Ambition and decide when to Unleash power. Push too far and Overreach can punish you.

Round Flow

1Set 1 monster
2Set 1 spell/trap if available
3Choose Strike, Guard or Focus
4Prepare Unleash if you have 5 Ambition
5Press Ready and resolve battle

Intents

Strike+150 clash power if you win. +100 extra damage if you lose.
GuardBlocks 350 incoming damage.
FocusGain +2 Ambition if you survive the round.

Ambition

Ambition is your risk resource. You gain it by playing aggressively, dealing damage and reading the elemental clash correctly.

UnleashSpend 5 Ambition for +300 battle power.
OverreachIf Ambition exceeds 10, take 350 damage and reset to 6.

Elements

Elemental advantage gives +300 power during monster clashes.

Firebeats Plant
Plantbeats Earth
Earthbeats Water
Waterbeats Fire

Sigils

Sigils are monster alignments. They connect your card, Intent and battle situation.

Fury+100 power with Strike.
Resolve+100 power with Guard.
Insight+1 Ambition with Focus.
Ruin+100 damage when winning with elemental advantage.
HarmonyBlocks 100 damage while in elemental disadvantage.

Deck Rules

Beta decks use 30 cards: 21 monsters, 6 spells and 3 traps.
