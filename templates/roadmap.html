Working Now

Playable core loop

Ascension Duel Training, Ambition Core, Chronicle, XP, Gold, daily rewards, missions, collection, deck builder, boosters and profile progression are active in beta. Legacy Arena remains available for fallback.

Still Beta

Progression may be tuned

Gold values, booster pulls, mission rewards, card balance and ranking criteria can change as more playtest data arrives.

Known Limits

No live economy or real-money store

Gold and boosters are internal testing systems. Multiplayer ranking, marketplace, trading and payment flows are not active.

Feedback

Report friction from the current screen

Use Beta Feedback for bugs, balance notes, visual issues, suggestions or praise. Reports are stored locally in the beta backend.

Next Cycles

Balance and onboarding data

The next work should inspect telemetry, match outcomes, deck readiness and booster unlocks before adding larger systems.

Beta Notice

Progress can be adjusted

Public beta data is useful for testing and may be reset, rebalanced or migrated before a full launch.

Beta notice

Gold is beta currency only. No payment flow is active.

Current rewards are defensive beta progression: XP, Gold, mission progress, collection visibility and deck guidance. Booster opening is being tested without real-money checkout.
