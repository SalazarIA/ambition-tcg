Ambitionz beta privacy notice

1. Overview

Ambitionz is an online tactical card game currently operating in beta. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected, why it is used, and how players can contact the project team.

2. Official Website

The official Ambitionz website is https://ambition-tcg.onrender.com.

3. Data We Store

Ambitionz may store account data such as username, email address, password hash, verification status, login activity, game progression, deck data, collection data, match history, feedback reports, tester status and operational logs.

4. Why We Use Data

Data is used to operate accounts, authenticate users, save game progress, run matches, provide rewards, improve balance, detect bugs, support beta testing, prevent abuse and maintain service stability.

5. Passwords

Passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes. Ambitionz does not store plain text passwords.

6. Email Usage

Email may be used for account verification, password reset, essential account communication, beta support and operational notices.

7. Gameplay and Balance Data

During beta, match records, deck information, progression, rewards, missions and feedback may be reviewed to improve gameplay, balance, onboarding and stability.

8. Feedback and Support

When a player submits feedback, Ambitionz may store the message, category, severity, page context, username and submission date so the team can review bugs, balance issues and user experience problems.

9. Third-Party Infrastructure

Ambitionz may use external hosting, database, email, analytics, deployment and infrastructure providers to operate the service. These providers may process technical data necessary to deliver the application.

10. Beta Resets

Because Ambitionz is in beta, test accounts, progression, cards, rewards, match data, logs and other gameplay data may be reset, changed or removed for development, balance, security or stability reasons.

11. Data Removal Requests

Players can request support or account/data review through the feedback page at https://ambition-tcg.onrender.com/feedback.

12. Contact

For privacy, support or beta-related requests, use the official feedback page: https://ambition-tcg.onrender.com/feedback.

13. Updates

This policy may be updated as Ambitionz evolves from beta testing toward public release.
