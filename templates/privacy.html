Data Collected

Ambition TCG stores account data such as username, email, password hash, cards, match history, feedback reports and gameplay statistics.

Purpose

Data is used to operate accounts, save game progress, improve balance, review feedback and maintain beta stability.

Passwords

Passwords are stored as hashes. The application does not store plain-text passwords.

Contact

During beta, privacy or account removal requests should be sent directly to the project owner.
