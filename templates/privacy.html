1. Data We Store

Ambitionz stores account data such as username, email, password hash, verification status, game progression, deck data, collection data, match data, feedback and operational logs.

2. Email Usage

Email is used for account verification, password reset and essential account-related messages.

3. Passwords

Passwords are stored as hashes, not as plain text.

4. Beta Operations

During beta, gameplay data, logs, missions, rewards and test accounts may be reset to improve stability and balance.

5. Third-Party Infrastructure

Ambitionz may use external hosting, database and email infrastructure to operate the service.

6. Contact

For beta support, contact the project administrator through the available testing channel.
